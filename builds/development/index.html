Patents

United States Patent #4511291

When I was involved in the offshore oil and gas services industry, we were hindered by the size of abrasive blasting pressure vessels that could be used on marine vessels. We needed a way to use bulk quantities of abrasives. I designed, built, and tested prototypes until I had a device that would solve the problem. This system temporarily converted bulk abrasive blasting pressure vessels into vacuum vessels that could draw abrasives from bulk containers for refilling. This reduced the crew size by 35 percent. Abrasive material costs were lowered by using bulk truck loads. We increased productivity by more than 200 percent by reducing downtime waiting on the refills of the small abrasive pressure vessels. Information on the patent can be found at google patent site: www.google.com/patents/US4511291
